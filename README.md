# 🎯 Talent Community Job Sponsorship

**[→ Live Demo](https://deckelsf.github.io/talent-community-sponsor)**

A product prototype for a new job sponsorship experience built on top of Workstream's Talent Community — a network of 500,000+ pre-qualified hourly workers. Instead of sponsoring jobs on Indeed and getting low-quality, expensive candidates, employers can sponsor directly on Workstream and get location-matched workers who show up.

![Talent Community Sponsor Screenshot](SponsoredJobs.png)

---

## The Problem

Workstream customers already pay to sponsor jobs on Indeed. The results are poor:

- **~18% show rate** — most candidates never respond or don't show up to interviews
- **$85 cost per interview** — expensive for outcomes that rarely convert
- No location filtering, no pre-qualification, no relationship with the candidate

---

## The Solution

Workstream has built a Talent Community of 500,000+ hourly workers who have opted in to find jobs. This prototype demonstrates a new sponsorship flow that lets employers put their job in front of that network — with location matching, faster response times, and dramatically lower cost per interview.

| Metric | Workstream Talent Community | Indeed Sponsored |
|---|---|---|
| Show rate | **62%** | 18% |
| Cost per interview | **$32** | $85 |
| Avg. response time | **2.1 hours** | 14+ hours |
| Location match | **Within 8 miles** | Unfiltered |

---

## Features

- **3 sponsorship modes** — set a budget, set a candidate goal, or set an interview goal
- **Live estimates** — results update in real time as you adjust your goal
- **Indeed comparison** — always-visible benchmark showing why Workstream delivers better ROI
- **Max budget cap** — optional guardrail so employers never overspend
- **4-step expanding flow** — select job → choose mode → set goal → review & approve
- **Confirmation + timeline** — post-approval screen showing when to expect candidates and interviews
- **Realistic prototype data** — pre-loaded with Chick-fil-A roles and location data to simulate a real customer session

---

## How to Use

1. Open the [live demo](https://deckelsf.github.io/talent-community-sponsor)
2. Select a job posting to sponsor
3. Choose your sponsorship mode — default is **interview goal**
4. Set how many interviews you want (adjust with +/− or type directly)
5. Review the live estimates and Indeed comparison
6. Click **Review & Approve** → **Confirm & Sponsor** to see the confirmation flow

---

## Context

This was built as a portfolio prototype to demonstrate product thinking around:

- **Marketplace dynamics** — connecting supply (Talent Community members) with demand (employer job postings) in a way that monetizes the network
- **Value-based pricing** — framing sponsorship around outcomes (interviews) rather than impressions or clicks
- **Competitive positioning** — making the ROI advantage over Indeed tangible and visible throughout the purchase flow
- **Conversion-focused UX** — progressive disclosure, live feedback, and social proof to reduce friction at each step
